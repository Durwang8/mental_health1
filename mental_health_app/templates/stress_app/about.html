About This Project

This Mental Health Assessment platform is designed to help parents and children assess and understand stress levels through scientifically backed questionnaires.

Built using Django and machine learning models, this tool aims to:

  • Provide easy stress evaluation for parents and children
  • Store and compare results between linked users (parent-child)
  • Promote open conversation about mental well-being within families

If you're a parent, you can register and link your child to assess both of your stress levels. The results are displayed on your dashboard for ongoing tracking and awareness.
